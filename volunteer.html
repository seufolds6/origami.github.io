I am the founder and manager of Folding for the Future (FFF), an online platform that connects origami groups on six continents interested in community service.

My Role in Folding for the Future


  • design and distribute community outreach ideas for the origami groups to try, like donating origami cards to thank front-line workers and starting online origami classes during the pandemic
  • provide my four book series about origami-inspired volunteering to each group leader
  • document the work of individual origami groups on the FFF website
  • encourage groups to spread awareness to other origami groups about origami as an outreach tool
